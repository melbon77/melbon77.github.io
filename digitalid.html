NYS Digital ID

UI • UX • Prototyping • Typography

Translating the traditional ID into a digital format.

Timeline - 6 weeks

Tools - Figma, Illustrator


View Process Deck (Google Drive PDF)

Overview

If you can do it with your money, why not with your identity?

Like storing a credit card securely in your digital wallet, this app provides quick and seamless access to a driver's license.

Visual Directions

1. Night Life - dark, dynamic, bubbly

2. Urban - minimal, clear, solid

3. Ticket - bold, simple, spirited

In the end, I drew most heavily from my first visual direction, creating a quiet, cozy, but dynamic feel.

Sketches

Exploration of how a design language that ties to New York State could be implemented as a simple interface.

Wireframes

In order to better protect a user's personal information, the digital ID can be in age-only or full ID view.

Iterations

My first iterations (yellow) were simple and flat, but not terrible. In further iterations I wanted to push the design to be more dynamic and drew more inspiration from my 1st visual design direction.

Final Designs

Prototype

This prototype goes through selecting an ID and viewing it in both age-only view and full view. It also shows the scannable QR code

With More Time...

There is SO much more I would like to do with this design.

I was just starting to create a brand identity for 'NYSID'; the direction could be pushed further and better fleshed out. Design language and color palette could be altered to assist with this.

The screen displaying all the user's licenses also needs more TLC. The buttons could look more like cards and less like a call to action.
