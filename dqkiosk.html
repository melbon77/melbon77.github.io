Dairy Queen Kiosk

UI • UX • Heuristic Evaluation

Designing a self-service kiosk for DQ locations.

Overview

Timeline: 15 weeks

Tools: Figma, Illustrator

Heuristic Evaluation

I tested kiosks in competing restaurants to find successful elements as well as common pitfalls.

I looked at kiosks at Taco Bell, Panera Bread, McDonald's, and took a deep-dive into Crumbl Cookies.

Workflow

Any choices a user may need to take were organized into work flows.

This informed what screens would be needed in the design.

Physical Mockup

A full scale model allowed me to determine appropriate font and button sizing.

Wireframes

Final Designs

Styling was modeled after the company's existing site.

Prototype

Next Steps

With more time to put towards this design, I would fully prototype user interactions; what page transitons would look like as well as the full checkout process.
