Better Rocks and Stones

3D Modeling • AR • Illustration

A mini zine with an interactive AR component

Overview

Better Rocks and Stones is a zine advertising punk rock jewelry. The name is a play on the widely known Better Homes & Gardens magazine.

Timeline - 3 weeks

Tools - Spline 3D, Meta Spark Studio, Procreate

Contributors - Melanie Boncaro (AR, 3D Modeling), Jenna DuBois (Illustration, 3D Modeling)

What is a Zine?

A zine (pronounced "zeen") is a small, homemade magazine, usually produced by an individual or small group. They often cover specific and unconventional subject matter.

Demo

The AR interaction was created using Meta Spark and is hosted publically on Instagram.
