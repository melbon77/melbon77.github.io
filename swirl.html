Your guide to cursive writing.

Overview

A digital platform for learning to write in cursive for those still interested.


Timeline: 6 weeks

Tools: Figma, Paper and pen

User Flow

Lo-Fi Sketches

Initial sketches were done in portrait orientation. Since the app's writing screen worked better in landscape, the rest of the screens were formatted horizontally.

Hi-Fi Designs

More developed sketches of the visual style paired with the layout.

Final Designs

Users are able to choose to practice writing individual letters or entire words.

Errors & Guidance

When a user presses the back button during a practice session, confirmation is requested before exiting.

Prototype

This prototype goes through a user's experience practicing letters and a word in cursive, demonstrates error correction and guidance, and shows the user's history data visualization.

With More Time...

I adore cursive for its artistic qualities, but my design feels a bit uninteresting.

I would love to give the interface a more unique and distinguishable style with a distict typeface and brand elements.
