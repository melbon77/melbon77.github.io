Your guide to cursive writing.

Overview

Timeline: 6 weeks

Tools: Figma, Illustrator

User Flow

Lo-Fi Sketches

Initial sketches were done in portrait orientation. Since the app's writing screen worked better in landscape, the rest of the screens were formatted horizontally.

Hi-Fi Designs

Exploration of how the transition from the full view of the ID to an age identification view could work.

Final Designs

My first iterations (yellow) were simple and flat, but not terrible. In further iterations I wanted to push the design to be more dynamic and drew more inspiration from my 1st visual design direction.

Errors & Guidance

When a user presses the back button during a practice session, confirmation is requested before exiting.

Prototype

This prototype goes through a user's experience practicing letters and a word in cursive, demonstrates error correction and guidance, and shows the user's history data visualization.

With More Time...

There is SO much more I would like to do with this design.

I was just starting to create a brand identity for 'NYSID'; the direction could be pushed further and better fleshed out. Design language and color palette could be altered to assist with this.

The screen displaying all the user's licenses also needs more TLC. The buttons should look more like 'cards' and less like a call to action.
