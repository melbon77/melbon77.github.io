Town of Webster, NY Site Redesign

UI/UX • Prototyping

Breathing new life into an overused format.

Overview

Once you've seen one city's website, you've seen them all.

Finding common shortfalls in this type of website and creating solutions.


Timeline: 10 weeks

Tools: Figma, Illustrator

Problems with the Current Design

  • Home page did not include much relevant content or give an idea of the town's character

  • Overwhelming sea of items in the navigation dropdowns

  • Unclear hierarchy in both navigation and content pages

Goals

  1. Create a better hierarchy to increase readability and skimmability.

  2. Give information more intentional organization.

  3. Establish a pleasing and cohesive style and color palette.

Final Designs

Final solutions for both the home page and Garbage & Recycling page.

How goals were addressed:

  1. Hierarchy: Section titles are larger and colored to stand out from subcontent.

  2. Organization: Home page content is relevant and informative. Garbage & Recycling page disposal providers are listed in individual sections and include provider-specific details.

  3. Style: More lively color palette. Leaf graphic motif used throughout site for style and continuity.
